    PpW Ale Grzeje

    "Ale Grzeje" (roughly translated as Heat Wave) is an upcoming event by PpW, scheduled for Saturday June 13th 2024. It's the second one to be hosted by Teatr Komuna, after Miasto Bezprawia.

    The announcement post featured the show's official poster, focusing on the term "Zmowa" (roughly translated as Collusion), promising the hottest (literally and figuratively) show of the summer. The hype text suggested "collusion versus collusion" - which refers to Biesiad's plan to exact revenge, and thwart Mister Z's and Gustav Gryffin's new alliance that screwed him of the PpW Championship belt at Ledwo Legalne 4.

    Prior to the show, PpW is holding a 5-day open seminar with Jacob Crane, which they call their "summer course".
