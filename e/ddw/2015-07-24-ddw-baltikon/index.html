    DDW @ Baltikon 2015

    This event was the final show held by Do Or Die Wrestling. It took place on Friday, July 27th, and was a part of Baltikon, a primarily anime and cosplay-focused fan convention, held since 2010 in Gdańsk. The 2015 edition was held in Sopot, in Zespół Szkół nr 1 which is a complex of schools at primary and secondary levels. It was also the final edition of the convention.
